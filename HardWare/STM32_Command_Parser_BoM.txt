
# Bill of Materials (BoM) for STM32 Command Parser and Control System

## Main Components
1. **STM32F401CC microcontroller** 
   - **Quantity**: 1
   - **Description**: Main microcontroller for UART, ADC, and GPIO handling.

2. **LM35 Temperature Sensor**
   - **Quantity**: 1
   - **Description**: Analog temperature sensor connected to ADC for temperature measurement.

3. **LDR (Light Dependent Resistor)**
   - **Quantity**: 1
   - **Description**: Analog sensor for light intensity measurement connected to ADC.

4. **Relay Module**
   - **Quantity**: 1
   - **Description**: Solid-state relay for controlling external devices, connected to GPIO.

## Interface Components

1. **Jumper Wires** for connections
   - **Quantity**: Varies (2-10)
   - **Description**: To connect peripherals and power supply to the microcontroller.

## Power Supply Components
1. **5V Power Supply Module**
   - **Quantity**: 1
   - **Description**: Provides stable 5V for microcontroller and peripherals.

## 10k Ohm Resistor
   - **Quantity**: 1
   - **Description**: Used for voltage division or pull-up/down in digital circuits.

## NPN Transistor (BC547)
   - **Quantity**: 1
   - **Description**: Used for switching applications in controlling relays or amplifying signals.
